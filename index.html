One-line install scripts.

Shell (zsh + oh-my-zsh + powerlevel10k)

curl -fsSL https://get.abd.dev/setup-shell.sh | bash

Installs zsh (if missing), oh-my-zsh, powerlevel10k, zsh-autosuggestions, zsh-syntax-highlighting, nvm + latest LTS node, uv, and the entire CLI. macOS and Ubuntu. Safe to re-run. Close and reopen your terminal when it finishes.
